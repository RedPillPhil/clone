Who is Slinky

Not   Your Average   Doge

A Slinky Doge finding his place on the Arbitrum Blockchain.

(For a more comprehensive outline of how the token works please refer to Bitcoin Talk)

Slinky Doge is an elastic supply meme token soon to be launched on the Arbitrum blockchain. With a starting supply of 100 Slinky and a near 0 market cap, Slinky Doge embarks on an exploration of tokenomics. Slinky Doge seeks to align its value with that of 1 USDT token through positive rebases whenever one Slinky exceeds the price of 1 USDT. Rebase percentages range from 1% to 10%, adjusting based on Slinky's relative value to USDT. In contrast to conventional rebase tokens, Slinky Doge implements a unique method for supply contraction without adversely affecting most holders. If Slinky falls below USDT's price, the token increases its burn rate on transactions. The default burn rate is set to 0.5%, but will begin escalating daily to a maximum of 10% when the supply needs to contract. This strategy shields average holders from negative rebases, while sellers bear the effects of a contracting supply. Join Slinky Doge on its insightful journey, where innovation converges with community to shape the future of token economics!
